Italian Meatball Recipe

Description

This is a meatball recipe that I learned from my best friend.

I always try to use fresh herbs when making this dish, as they really impact the flavor.

Also I usually substitute in oat milk and potatoe bread, due to my girlfriend's food allergies.

So feel free to play around with the ingredients in this recipe and see what works.

Ingredients

  • 3lbs Ground Beef
  • 2 Slices White Bread
  • 2 Eggs
  • 1 Cup Milk
  • 1/2 Cup Basil
  • 1/2 Cup Thyme
  • 1/2 Cup Oregano
  • 1/2 Cup Parsley
  • 2 Tbsp Vegetable Oil
  • 2 Jars of Tomato Sauce
  • Salt & Pepper to Taste

Recipe

  1. Rip apart the bread into small strips and soak in milk.
  2. In a large bowl, combine ground beef, eggs and soaked bread. Make sure not to add the excess milk to the meat.
  3. Add half of the chopped basil, thyme, oregano and parsley to the meat. Keep the other half to the side.
  4. Form small meatballs and set aside on a large plate or baking pan.
  5. Over medium-high heat, brown meatballs on all sides in a large, cast iron skillet.
  6. Once the meatballs are browned, mix both jars of tomato sauce and the rest of the herbs in the cast iron with the meatballs.
  7. Cover the pan and braise the meatballs over medium-low heat, for 30 minutes or until they reach an internal temperature of 165 F.
  8. Once the meatballs are finished, salt and pepper the sauce to taste and serve with fresh pasta. Enjoy!
