Chocolate Chip Cookie Recipe

Description

This is a Chocolate Chip Cookie recipe that I found on joyfoodsunshine.com.

I've used this recipe for years and the cookies always come out fantastic!

However, they are super rich, so you could always cut back on the sugar in the recipe.

Ingredients

  • 1 Cup Salted Butter
  • 1 Cup Sugar
  • 1 Cup Brown Sugar
  • 2 tsp Vanilla Extract
  • 2 Large Eggs
  • 3 Cups All-Purpose Flour
  • 1 tsp Baking Soda
  • 1/2 tsp Baking Powder
  • 1 tsp Sea Salt
  • 2 Cups of Chocolate Chips

Steps

  1. Cream together the butter and sugars until they are well combined and smooth.
  2. Beat in the eggs and add the vanilla extract.
  3. In a separate bowl, mix the flour, baking powder, baking soda and salt.
  4. Then slowly mix the dry ingredients into the wet mixture until the dough is formed.
  5. Add the chocolate chips to the dough, then portion out small balls of dough onto a baking pan.
  6. Preheat oven to 375 F and bake the cookies for 8-10 minutes, or until they just start to brown.
